What is NPP?

National People's Power (NPP) is a political movement made up of 21 different groups, including political parties, youth groups, women's groups, trade unions, and other civil society organisations. It was established in 2019 as a result of a series of conversations about the need for a progressive political platform.

These include a non-corrupt, service-oriented, accountable, and transparent political culture; economic democracy with greater participation in the economy and fairer distribution of economic benefits; strong social protections; and an inclusive, democratic Sri Lankan identity.
